# PowerBI-Projects
* Designed and implemented advanced BI systems across financial risk modeling, sales optimization, retail intelligence, and energy analytics to transform complex datasets into structured decision-support frameworks.
* Built scalable star-schema data models, developed structured ETL pipelines using Power Query, and implemented advanced DAX measures including MTD, MoM, YTD, variance analysis, KPI benchmarking, and dynamic segmentation.
* Applied financial modeling, risk categorization, trend and seasonality analysis, volatility measurement, contribution analysis, and concentration risk assessment to uncover deeper performance insights.
* Enabled profitability validation, exposure quantification, revenue optimization, operational efficiency monitoring, infrastructure utilization analysis, and data-driven expansion planning.
* Translated multi-dimensional business data into executive-level dashboards designed for measurable outcomes, strategic planning, and performance-driven decision-making.
